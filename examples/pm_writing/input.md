# Product Brief: Unified Notifications

## Problem

Users miss important updates because alerts are scattered across email, in-app
banners, and a separate mobile push system. This creates a disjointed and
frustrating experience that leverages three different notification stacks
for what should be one coherent signal.

## Proposal

Utilize a single notification service that both platforms call in order to
deliver every alert. Rather than pursuing a synergy between the existing
systems, we replace them outright.

## Why this is best-in-class

Our early prototype is a cutting-edge, revolutionary approach that will be
disruptive to how competitors handle notifications, and it delivers
significant gains in delivery latency and a substantial drop in duplicate
alerts.

## Rollout

We may ship the unified system alongside the Q3 mobile release, pending
confirmation from the platform team on timeline.
